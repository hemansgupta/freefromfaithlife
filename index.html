Our Mission

Healing is not a luxury.

FreeFromFaith believes mental wellness is a birthright. We bring trauma-informed yoga, meditation, and peer support to communities across Canada — completely free.

Secular. Inclusive. Accessible. No matter your background, belief, or income.
